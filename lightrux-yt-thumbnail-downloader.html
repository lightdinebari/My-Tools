What is the highest quality YouTube thumbnail size?
The maximum resolution YouTube thumbnail is called "maxresdefault" and is 1280×720 pixels (HD). Not all videos have this size available — if it is not found, the next best is "hqdefault" at 480×360 pixels.
Can I download thumbnails from YouTube Shorts?
Yes. This tool supports YouTube Shorts links (youtube.com/shorts/VIDEO_ID) as well as standard watch links and youtu.be short links.
Is it legal to download YouTube thumbnails?
YouTube thumbnails are publicly accessible images. Downloading them for personal use, research, or reference is generally acceptable. However, using another creator's thumbnail commercially or claiming it as your own may infringe on their copyright. Always respect content creators' rights.
Why is the Max HD thumbnail not available for some videos?
Not all YouTube videos have a 1280×720 maxresdefault thumbnail. Older videos or those uploaded in lower quality may only have SD or HD thumbnails. This tool automatically shows whichever sizes are available.
How do I save the thumbnail on mobile?
On mobile devices, tap the Download button. If your browser opens the image instead of saving it, press and hold the image and select "Save Image" or "Download Image" from the menu.
