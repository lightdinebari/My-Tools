💡 Meta Tag Best Practices

    

      
1

      
Keep title under 60 characters — anything longer gets truncated in Google search results.

    

    

      
2

      
Meta description: 120–160 characters — this is your ad copy in search results. Make it count.

    

    

      
3

      
Always add Open Graph tags — controls how your page looks when shared on Facebook, WhatsApp & LinkedIn.

    

    

      
4

      
Use canonical URLs — prevents duplicate content issues, especially important for Blogger pages with ?m=1 URLs.

    

    

      
5

      
Keywords meta tag is ignored by Google — but still used by Bing and Yahoo, so it's worth adding.
