💡 YouTube Title Tips
1

Keep it under 60 characters — titles get cut off in search results beyond that.

2

Put your main keyword first — YouTube's algorithm weights the beginning of your title more.

3

Use numbers — "7 Ways to…" outperforms generic titles because they set clear expectations.

4

Create curiosity gaps — "The #1 mistake most people make with…" drives clicks naturally.

5

Test multiple titles — use A/B testing with YouTube's built-in feature after uploading.
